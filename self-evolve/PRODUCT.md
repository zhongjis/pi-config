# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Technical operators triaging Pi agent sessions. They want to identify failing patterns, compare baseline vs. new configurations, and understand cost/duration outliers. They value data density over narrative.

## Product Purpose
A dense read-only dashboard to evaluate self-evolve Module 1 extraction runs. It surfaces extraction health, aggregate metrics, and explicit baseline comparisons without inventing confidence where data is missing.

## Brand Personality
Restrained, technical, transparent.

## Anti-references
- Brand marketing sites
- Playful or whimsical UI (e.g., PostHog)
- Finance/Trading dashboards (e.g., Kraken)
- "AI Magic" glowing/animated recommendations

## Design Principles
- **Data Density:** Show the table, expose the metrics.
- **Comparison Clarity:** Clear visual diffs for metrics (baseline vs. target).
- **No Faux Certainty:** If a metric or similarity measure is missing/unknown, say so. Do not invent confidence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Ensure high contrast for status indicators (Success/Fail/Warning).
- Provide keyboard-navigable session list and comparison selection.
- Screen reader friendly table structures.
